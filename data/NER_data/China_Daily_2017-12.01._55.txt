Sovereign bonds worth 6.5b yuan issued in HK The Ministry of Finance has issued an aggregate 6.5 billion yuan ($984 million) of sovereign bonds in Hong Kong, including 4 billion yuan of two-year bonds carrying a coupon rate of 3.9 percent, the Hong Kong Monetary Authority said on Thursday. The finance ministry also issued 2 billion yuan of five-year bonds with a coupon rate of 4.10 percent, and 500 million yuan of 10-year bonds carrying a coupon rate of 4.15 percent. The ministry has annually issued dim sum bonds in Hong Kong since 2009 to boost the development of the offshore market.
