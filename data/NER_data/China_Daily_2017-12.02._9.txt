Nation's progress impresses visitors
Delegates of parties from other countries who visited an exhibition in Beijing on Friday said they were amazed by China's technological improvements and they expect full exchanges and a closer relationship with the Communist Party of China. More than 200 of the delegates arrived at the Beijing Exhibition Center for an exhibition on China's outstanding achievements over the past five years as part of the CPC in Dialogue with World Political Parties High-Level Meeting. The meeting, organized by the International Department of the Central Committee of the CPC, is being held through Sunday.
