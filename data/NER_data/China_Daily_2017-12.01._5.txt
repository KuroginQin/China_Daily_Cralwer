Events and stories coming up in the next few days Fortune Global Forum to be held in Guangdong The Fortune Global Forum 2017 will be held in Guangzhou, Guangdong province, from Wednesday to Friday under the theme of "Openness and Innovation: Shaping the Global Economy". The event will bring together CEOs from the world's biggest companies - the Fortune Global 500 - with leaders from China in both business and government.
