Raheem reprises rescue role
Pep singing Sterling's praises after another late City winner MANCHESTER, England-Raheem Sterling was hailed as a "special" player by his Manchester City manager Pep Guardiola after another of the forward's late winners sent the Etihad Stadium crowd into delirium on Wednesday. With almost the last kick of the game, Sterling's exquisite 96th-minute curler earned City a 2-1 win over Southampton.
