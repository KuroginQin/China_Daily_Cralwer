Xi: Joint efforts key to shared future
At dialogue of world's parties, president vows CPC will help safeguard global peace, stability Xi Jinping, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee, called on political parties worldwide on Friday to make joint efforts to build a community of shared future for mankind as the Communist Party of China, the world's largest political party with 89 million members, held the first high-level dialogue with world political parties in Beijing. Xi, also president, made the remarks in his keynote speech at the opening ceremony of the CPC in Dialogue with World Political Parties High-Level Meeting at the Great Hall of the People.
